My Own Face Id App



Welcome to the Emotion Recogniser! In case you don't know how to use this app here are the instructions: Pose your face near so the the webcam can view it. Act out one of the emotions the model is trained to recognise. When you are ready click Capture Image. After that, simply press check and the computer will run the emotion throught the system! Enjoy!

Silly
Happy

Serious
